Research Summary

Our lab, established in early 2020, has been dedicated to acheiving highly discriminative and automated individualized precision care through the application of machine learning and data science using clinical patient health information, medical imaging, clinician notes, and high fidelity bedside monitoring data.

Joining the Lab

For Students

LCICM offers research opporunities to Johns Hopkins University and Johns Hopkins School of Medicine students, both through the medical tutorial program as well as independent projects for those motivated.

We highly recommend potential students to have prerequisite background in machine learning, statistics, data science, and some level of computer science/programming knowledge. A display of such knowledge through a prior project and/or industry experience is always welcomed.

For Collaborators

For multi-disciplinary collaborators within the JHU community interested in utilizing the expertise of LCICM faculty, staff, and/or students to improve modeling in your respective departments, please contact us.

A degree is required of anyone outside of the JHU community interested in collaborating with the laboratory. For those with a degree interested in collaborating with the laboratory, please contact us.
